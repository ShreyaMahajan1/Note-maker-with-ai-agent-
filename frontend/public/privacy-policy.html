Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Notes App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you register, we collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Password (encrypted)

2.2 Notes Data

We store the notes you create, including:

  • Note content
  • Note metadata (color, category, timestamps)
  • Links you add to notes

2.3 Google Calendar Data

If you connect your Google Calendar, we access:

  • Calendar events (read and write access)
  • OAuth tokens to maintain the connection

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Create and manage your account
  • Store and sync your notes
  • Create calendar events from your notes (if you enable this feature)
  • Provide AI-powered features (note suggestions, enhancements, categorization)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt
  • Data is stored in MongoDB with encryption
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • We use HTTPS for all data transmission

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Calendar API: To create and manage calendar events (only if you connect your calendar)
  • Groq AI: To provide AI-powered note suggestions and enhancements
  • Google Gemini AI: For video transcription features
  • MongoDB: For data storage

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Disconnect your Google Calendar at any time
  • Export your notes data

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All your notes are permanently deleted
  • Your account information is removed
  • OAuth tokens are revoked and deleted

8. Google Calendar Permissions

When you connect your Google Calendar, we request permission to:

  • View and create calendar events
  • Update events created by our app
  • Delete events created by our app

We only access your calendar when you explicitly connect it. You can disconnect at any time, and we will immediately stop accessing your calendar and delete all stored tokens.

9. AI Features

Our AI features process your note content to provide:

  • Note suggestions and enhancements
  • Automatic categorization
  • Meeting summaries
  • Video transcription

Your data is sent to third-party AI providers (Groq, Google Gemini) for processing but is not stored by them.

10. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal tracking:

  • JWT tokens stored in localStorage for authentication
  • No third-party tracking cookies
  • No analytics or advertising cookies

11. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at:

Email: support@yourapp.com

14. Compliance

This privacy policy complies with:

  • Google API Services User Data Policy
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
